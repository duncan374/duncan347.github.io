Tournaments

Tournaments are many member's favorite part of being on the team.We compete in two to three water ski tournaments usually during the fall semester. This is an amazing opportunity to network with people all over the state and country. Relations made during these social and competitive gatherings last for a lifetime!

  • Compete against schools all over the Midwest and Nation while representing Eau Claire!
  • There are three events: Slalom, Trick, and Jump
  • Tournaments are a fun time to bond with teammates and meet skiers from competing schools
The Waterski and Wakeboard Team is a recognized Club Sports team at the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire
